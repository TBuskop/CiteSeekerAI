About Collect Abstracts

Enter a Scopus search string to collect academic abstracts. The system will search Scopus, download matching abstracts, and store them in the database for analysis with CiteSeekerAI.

This step must be performed before asking research questions about a new topic.

Examples:
• ("water scarcity") AND (agriculture OR farming) AND climate
• ("food security") AND "changing climate" AND NOT disease
Use quotes for phrases, AND/OR/NOT operators, and parentheses for complex queries.
Select where Scopus should look for your search terms. "ALL" is broader and includes search of keywords in cited paper titles, "TITLE-ABS-KEY" is more focused to the paper itself.
